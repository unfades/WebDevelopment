Getting Started With Bouldering

How to get started

Unlike many outdoor sports, getting started with bouldering is surprisingly simple. All you need are
some climbing shoes, chalk, and a positive attitude—most gyms even rent the equipment for your
first sessions. No prior experience or complex gear required!

The simple steps to getting started

  1. Find a bouldering gym nearby.
  2. Rent or buy some climbing shoes.
  3. Learn basic bouldering techniques.
  4. Start with easy routes, slowly up the pace.
  5. Join a bouldering community or club.
